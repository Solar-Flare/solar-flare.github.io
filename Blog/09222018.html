Gravitation, Big Crunch (UD Part 2)

Date: 09/21/2018

Part 2 of Universe Death Series as illustrated here.

Gravity pulls all things with mass together. You are attracting the sun right now, and so is a tiny electron, and the earth is pulling you towards it, allowing us to stay on the surface and never leave.


When the Big Bang occurred, everything ejected in all directions at massive velocities.

But even as they were moving away from each other, they only had one acceleration: gravity. And theoretically, if the stellar objects are not moving at a fast enough speed, eventually gravity will stop it to a halt. And then the objects will start moving towards each other.

Eventually all the matter will gravitate towards the center of the universe, and generate another singularity. This is the Big Crunch.

This is also the only theoretical universe ending that supports multiple universes in the same timeline! As a big bang of the singularity created our universe as is currently, and this ending creates another singularity, it is plausible that it will explode again, creating a whole new universe!

Or it can just stay there and be a good singularity. It can do whatever it wants, right?
