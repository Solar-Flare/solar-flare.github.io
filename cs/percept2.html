Perceptron 2: Half-Moon

Date: 08/01/2018

Not an Edible Crescent Moon

This is a modification to the first perceptron in which the data set, instead of being user-input, is replaced by a 2000-point set, where 1000 points are red and 1000 points are blue and are both shaped like a half moon. It is very slightly linearly separateable, but barely.
This serves as a way of making sure the perceptron is at least really capable of linear classification.
